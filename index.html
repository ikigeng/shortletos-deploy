Instrument 02

Tourism Dirham Calculator

Tourism Dirham is charged per occupied bedroom, per night: AED 10 for a Standard unit, AED 15 for a Deluxe unit. It is collected from the guest and remitted to DET — it is not VAT.

Note: for a single stay longer than 30 consecutive nights, Tourism Dirham applies to the first 30 nights of that stay only. Enter the nights that were actually charged.
